How to Get Support

Opening a ticket is quick and easy. Choose your preferred method below and our system will log your request immediately.

email
Email us: help@cyberjo.tech
Ticket confirmation details - cyberjo.on.spiceworks.com

We use Spiceworks for ticketing. You will receive a confirmation email from help@cyberjo.on.spiceworks.com containing your ticket number. Please reply to that email to add updates to your ticket. (help@cyberjo.co.za also works)

chat
WhatsApp us: +27 21 001 5490
qr_code
Scan QR code: Open QR image
Scan this QR code to start a WhatsApp chat with our support team.

What happens next?

  • A ticket will be created automatically.
  • One of our technicians will contact you by email or WhatsApp.
  • We will manage and escalate the ticket as required until the issue is resolved.
